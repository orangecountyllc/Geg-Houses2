Mike Butler    

Mike Butler has over 30 years of experience across a range of senior executive roles in the healthcare industry. He was formerly President of Providence , where he helped to create the third largest health system in the U.S., increasing revenue from $2 billion when he joined in 1998 to $25 billion today.

During Mr. Butler’s tenure, he helped architect a contiguous growth strategy that allowed Providence to build connected networks of care across the Western U.S. His commitment to delivering health care across scale helped pave the way for clinical improvements, innovation and affordability.

He also helped advance key strategies around population health, digital innovation, clinical institutes, ambulatory care, medical groups and revenue diversification. His legacy includes spearheading a commitment to ending homelessness in the Western U.S. and leading international service trips to Guatemala.

He was instrumental in helping the ministry grow to serve more people through mergers, acquisitions, divestitures, contiguous community growth and internal regional consolidation, resulting in the financial viability needed to ensure our Mission flourishes for generations to come.

Over his tenure, Providence brought on new partners, including Swedish Health Systems, PacMed, Kadlec Regional Medical Center, Saint John’s Health Center, Encino-Tarzana Regional Medical Center, Facey Medical Group and St. Joseph Health.
