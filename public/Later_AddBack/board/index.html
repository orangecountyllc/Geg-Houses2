Timeline of the process

Step 1. Introductory phone call ( 1 week)
We chat about your Business.

Step 1. Zoom call ( 1 - 2 weeks )
We will meet on a zoom call and get to know about your goals, and what is important to you.

Step 2. Initial Proposal ( 1 - 2 weeks )
We will present our initial Proposal, just a few high-level things.

Step 3. Final Proposal ( 1-2 weeks )
We will present a detailed summary of the acquisition structure tailored to you, and your Business.
( No cookie-cutter approach. )

Step 4. Deeper exploration of the Business, your goals and future alignment ( multiple weeks )
We want to know more about your Business, like the number of active patients, revenue numbers, etc.

Step 5. Partnership ( 1-3 weeks )
If we are a match, we will create an acquisition proposal for you.
