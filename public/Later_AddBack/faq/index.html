Why us


No matter where your practice falls in terms of its current lifecycle, Equity Group offer solutions that are aligned with the needs of your business, your life and your future.

We are a new firm and that enables us to be more flexible than large established firms and provide personalized service to you, based upon your individual goals and desires.

Unlike other firms, we are committed to building a real partnership between our management team and practitioners; “learning what drives you and what you see as your key goals and challenges”.



Timeline of the partnership process



Step 1. Introductory Call Exploration ( 1 week )
We chat about your practice and who else works there.

Step 1. Zoom call ( 1 - 2 weeks )
We will meet on the zoom call and we get to know each other's goal and most importantly we want to know what is important to you.
And if we have an alignment with culture.

Step 2. Initial Proposal ( 1 - 2 weeks )
We will present to you with our initial proposal.
Our Board of Directors will personally present a detailed summary of the partnership agreement tailored to you and your practice.

Step 3. Final Proposal. ( 1-2 weeks )
Our experts will present a detailed summary of the partnership agreement tailored to you and your practice.

Step 4. Deeper exploration of the Practice and it's growth potential ( multiple weeks )
Our experts will want to know more about your practice like number of active patients and revenue numbers.

Step 5. Partnership ( 1-3 weeks )
We will prepare you and your practice to join the Equit Dental Parters in a non-disruptive way.
When we have an alignment in the vistion, we will create a partnership proposal within one to three weeks.
