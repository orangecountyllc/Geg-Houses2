Timeline of the process

Step 1. Introductory Call Exploration Phase ( 1 weeks )
We chat about your practice and who else works there.

Step 1. Zoom call ( 1 - 2 weeks )
We will meet on the zoom call and we get to know each other's goal and most importantly we want to know what is important to you. If we have an alignment with culture.

Step 2. Initial Proposal ( 1–2 weeks ) ( 1 - 2 weeks )
We will present to you with our initial proposal. Our Board of Directors will personally present a detailed summary of the partnership agreement tailored to you and your practice.

Step 3. Final Proposal. ( 1-2 weeks )
Our experts will present a detailed summary of the partnership agreement tailored to you and your practice.

Step 4. Deeper exploration of the Practice and it's growth potential ( multiple weeks )
Our experts will want to know more about your practice like number of active patients and revenue numbers.

Step 5. Partnership ( 1-3 weeks )
We will prepare you and your practice to join the Equit Dental Parters in a non-disruptive way. When we have an alignment in the vistion, we will create a partnership proposal within one to three weeks.
