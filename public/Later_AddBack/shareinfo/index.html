Minimum criteria for acquisition and ways to increase the value of a practice.


1) Practice should have a minimum of 6 operatories and ~850K in revenue.

In a typical practice, a Dentist brings 70% of collections, and a Hygienist brings 30% of the collections

2) We can increase the value of the practice in the following ways :
a) By increasing the number of associates and thus increasing the number of patients operated at the practice.
b) If the current owner only works on a pay per service, we can increase the collection by taking the insurance.
But we have to be mindful that the practice has enough active patient flow.

Questions asked by sellers and our response.


  • Seller: My practice is different than others. It's more like a Clear choice DSO ( Clear choice just focuses on dental implants/ Dentures). Are you interested in buying my practice?
    Answer: Yes

  • If the practice collection numbers are good (800k+ ) but have only five operatories ( we look for minimum six operatories ) & the seller is willing to relocate to a closeby location to meet our criteria?

    Answer: As long as the seller is willing to invest in it. We'll be happy to pay $X per customer if the patient comes back for at least two visits.

  • What if everybody in practice speaks their own language? ( Asian owner)
    Answer: Yes

  • The seller pays all his associates per diem ( contract ). Should we buy it?
    Answer: yes

  • The seller has hired all his associates (students) from the university in California. And they require a full-time dentist for the associates to work. Should we buy it?
    Answer: yes, As long as the seller takes the responsibility of being available at the practice every day.
    Generally, we stay away from academia, and they're usually not the highest producing dentists.

  • Seller ( Dr. Minh from Visalia, CA ): Will you buy my medical supply company?
    Answer: No

  • Seller: I don't want to keep working in the practice. I have associates who will work for you.
    I am getting old now, and I don't want to catch coronavirus.
    You just give me my money, and I don't need any responsibility.
    You are the new owner, so you should take care of all the responsibilities.
    I have worked for 40 years, and now I want to spend all my time with my family and relax.
    Eventually, I convinced him to work for a minimum of 4 years.

    Answer: I think we should use the callback clause here.
    We don't mind if the seller doesn't come to practice, but if the collection numbers go down ( which they will because the lead dentist has left ), he will not get the full payment.
