Application Progress

Mobile Application Update

Initially, we will use this mobile app internally for managing our practices and then dominate the worldwide market for dentists and veterinary practices.

Purpose:

It will help spread our brand name and bring more acquisition targets.

Purpose of the Application :
a) I want to emphasize that our primary work at Equity is to do a lot of acquisitions, not to sell software/ applicatoin.

The application will provide us with accountability and a bird's eye view on the performance of each practice.
b) Why are we making our own application ?
All the current dental practice management softwares are outdated.
They are stand alone installations on local computers. There is no simple way to get remote access. When I was conducting interviews of the CXOs, they told me,

"I wish I could show the dentists that they should focus more on these revenue-producing procedures but I can't do that in these softwares."
c) Status of software: Core funtionalities i.e database, user interface templates have been created. ( 70% architecture done) I do not spend any more time on it. After the first acquisition, I'll hire a team of developers and resume working on it.
Screenshot of what people think of current dental practice mgmnt softwares



Current Practice Management Softwares do not have metrics for managing multiple locations.



App features.


  • Early Review capture: After the visit, before the customer leaves the practice, the front desk person will assess the patient. With a click of a button, if the patient is happy, we send him a link to the google reviews to write a review, and if he's not satisfied, we send him an internal link to provide a customer review about his /her experience.
    Then somebody from our team will immediately call the customer and try to resolve the issue.
    This will save us from bad reviews on yelp.
    And we'll track how long it took for the practice manager to call the patient(bad reviewer).
    There will be a lot more measures in the application that will help us to avoid becoming: Aspen Dental
  • Internal review system for new associates:
    Based on various KPIs, we will know if he/she's the right fit.
  • Doctor performance: We aren't afraid to tell people where they stand. Doctors will have appraisals not quarterly but daily.
    .
  • Associates will be able to track their goals, and we can support them to help reach the bonus they desire.
    Associates can compare their own KPI graphs with the top performer.
    We need to tell them their strengths and weaknesses and how they can work to improve.
  • Everybody in practice knows what the monthly revenue target is, and upon achieving their target, everybody will receive a bonus.
